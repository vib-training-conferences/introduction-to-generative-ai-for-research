## Question 1

**The course material describes a spectrum of AI integration in academic writing. Which category best describes a process where a researcher writes the initial draft, and an AI tool provides feedback, suggestions for improvement, or peer review enhancement?**

## Question 3

**When discussing the ethics of uploading documents to AI tools, the material mentions a legal doctrine that may permit using copyrighted works for teaching, scholarship, or research. What is this doctrine called?**

- [( )] A) Intellectual property concern  
- [( )] B) Creative Commons License  
- [(X)] C) FAIR use  
- [( )] D) VIB AI Policy

<details><summary>Hint</summary>
This concept balances copyright holders’ rights with the public interest in wider access for **teaching, scholarship, or research**.
</details>

<details><summary>Explain/Answer</summary>
**Correct:** C — *Fair use.*  
(Spelled “Fair use” in most references.) It can allow limited use of copyrighted material without permission under specific conditions for education, research, commentary, etc. Always check your jurisdiction and institutional guidance.
</details>
